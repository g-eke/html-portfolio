The Best Movies According to Dr. G

My Top Three Series of all time

The Neighborhood

The show follows Dave Johnson, portrayed by Max Greenfield, who is described as the "nicest guy in the Midwest." He moves his family into a predominantly black neighborhood, where they encounter various challenges and humorous situations as they try to fit in. The series explores themes of friendship, cultural differences, and community dynamics, particularly through the interactions between Dave and his next-door neighbor, Calvin Butler, played by Cedric the Entertainer, who is a local homeowner and the unofficial "mayor" of the neighborhood.

Designated Survivor

Designated Survivor" is a political thriller TV series that follows Thomas Kirkman, a low-level Cabinet member who unexpectedly becomes President of the United States after a catastrophic attack kills everyone above him in the line of succession. The series begins with an explosion during the State of the Union address, which decimates the U.S. government and leaves Secretary of Housing and Urban Development, Thomas Kirkman (played by Kiefer Sutherland), as the only survivor in the presidential line of succession. As he is thrust into the role of President, Kirkman must navigate the complexities of leadership, uncover the truth behind the attack, and manage various political crises while dealing with his inexperience.

How to get away with murder

How to Get Away with Murder is an American legal drama-thriller created by Peter Nowalk and produced by Shonda Rhimes under the Shondaland banner. It premiered on ABC on September 25, 2014, and concluded on May 14, 2020, spanning six seasons and 90 episodes.
